About

Masters in Computer Science candidate | Researcher | Artist

Who is Lora?

I am an aspiring software developer and UX/UI designer who apply art, science, and technology to discover and enhance tools to improve efficiency of everyday lives.

Growing up, I have always loved art, music, histology, and technology. My love for the arts and the sciences was further enhanced when I transitioned to college. I started doodling around 2015 after taking my Organic Chemistry exam and thinking I was going to fail…. Fortunately, I didn’t. Around the same year, I then became an avid fan of Histology or the study of tissue using a microscope because of the artistic and complex structures formed by the different tissue of the body. This pushed me to pursue my career in research.

Although I took computer science courses in college, my interest in technology increased after becoming a Research Assistant. I was introduced to the power of problem solving and automation, which pushed me to begin my path in computer science. From a self-taught doodler to a researcher, I dove into my journey of what I title as “problem solving with style”.

Through my past coursework and personal projects, I’ve further developed an interest in trying to fuse my appreciation in the arts, sciences, and technology. Now I desire to continue my passion and seek new opportunities to expand my knowledge and experiences.
